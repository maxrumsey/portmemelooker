PortMemeLooker

Port Meme Looker. Your solution to mobile-device oriented memes being viewed from a desktop.

Ever see a meme on your favourite meme site, that tells you to view it from your mobile device? Don't bother opening your phone or pulling up Paint, just Copy Image Address and put it into the below form.

How To Use

  1. Put the URL of the image into the Image URL box.
  2. Press the Set Image URL button.
  3. Change the X and Y sliders until the text becomes visible!
